IoT based Parking System

This parking system utilized LED's, IR Sensors and an Arduino Microcontroller coded in C. The system was connected to an IoT based application Blynk, allowing the user to know which parking slot is empty in real-time. This project was awarded the "Most Popular Choice" in the UOWD Innovation Fair in 2022.

AI-Based Dementia Glasses

This prototype involving constructing a wearable spectacles for dementia patients, to aid them in their daily lives. Using the AlexNet CNN in MATLAB, the glasses would take a video input and recognise the person infront of the user. The person's name and their relation to the user would be displayed on the glass of the frame to aid the user in recognizing their close ones, which start of as an early side effect of dementia.

DL for Efficient & Robust Meteor Detection Systems

This was a final year thesis project, which utilized CNN models to explore the merit of current meteor detection systems which are used in many space research centers such as the SAASST, EXOSS, BRAMON, BOAM & UKMON. This was awarded as the "Best Innovation" in the research category of the UOWD Innovation Fair in 2024.
